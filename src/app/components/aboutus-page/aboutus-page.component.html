CEO Message

To start with, we need to go little past to 1956 , the year our grant father started this seashell merchandise and are proud to tell you that we are now the 3rd GENERATION in the family into this spiritual and holy business for 65 years. We are famously called in rameswaram as “MOHAMMED ALIYAR FAMILY” in the island.

We are indigenous people of this island, and known for the shopping of Sea-Shell, Pearls & Spiritual Items in both RETAIL, WHOLESALE throughout PAN INDIA & GLOBAL .

We have branches operate across GERMANY, JAPAN, MALAYSIA, SINGAPORE, SRILANKA, UNITED KINGDOM , USA (Refer contact page for the same)

We operate from RAMESWARAM , which is one of the spiritual place and vibrant island where the seashell are brought from the SEASHORE directly. We process these seashells from its raw nature, clean and make it ready for the spiritual and aesthetic usage for temples, homes and into gracious wearable garlands, rosaries, pendants – refer the link below to see the processing of seashells from our company.

We are specialised in this core business and personally sorting out the best quality Sea-Shell for the customers for which we have a team of skilled personals specialized in such activities. All the final goods are tested by them before being offered to customers across the globe.

1956 – In the era of new independent India, at the corner of tamilnadu a young guy “MOHAMMED ALIYAR” started the seashell and pearl business from the historical place keelakarai, which is a flourishing seaport and a gateway to places where the international trade happened until the early 20th century.

1980 – The eldest of the Mohammed aliyar’s family and the proactive champ JAHABARDEEN enhanced the business with the extension of its trade throughout PAN INDIA and shifted the business to the communal hormonal place RAMESWARAM.

Then the business slowly increased in the local markets and as well the international locations late in 2000’s. The entire supply chain of the seashells in the local markets in rameswaram and the shops in and around procured the seashells and other spiritual items from the single source of our company “JAHABARDEEN SEA SHELL”
